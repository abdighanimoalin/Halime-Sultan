Who is Halime Hatun

Halime Hatun (not to be confused with Halime Sultan) was a seljuk noble woman from the 13th century who gave up her title to be with her husband ertugrul which she had 3 childern with Gunduz Bey, Saru Batu Savci Bey, and Osman Bey who went on to become the founder of the Ottoman Empire. Halime died at 1281, and contrary to popular belief Halime did NOT die while giving birth to Osman she lived a long life dying only a year after Ertugrul died.
